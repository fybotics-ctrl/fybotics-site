FYBOTICS builds affordable robotics systems and engineering solutions for education, research, and real-world deployment. From hardware to embedded systems, we turn engineering ideas into working machines.

What is FYBOTICS?

FYBOTICS is a robotics and engineering company focused on accessible robotic technologies and end-to-end engineering solutions combining mechanical design, electronics, embedded systems, and software.

Robotics Should Be Accessible

Advanced robotics is often expensive and inaccessible. FYBOTICS bridges this gap with practical, affordable engineering systems for students, startups, and research labs.
