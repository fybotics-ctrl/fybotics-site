What is FYBOTICS?

A robotics company built from practical products upward.

FYBOTICS is a robotics and engineering company focused on creating accessible robotic technologies and providing end-to-end engineering solutions. We combine mechanical design, electronics, embedded systems, software, and AI to develop products that help students, startups, researchers, and industries bring ideas into reality.

Our goal is simple: make robotics hardware practical, affordable, and useful for real prototyping, education, and engineering workflows.

Problem

Robotics should be accessible.

Advanced robotics platforms are often expensive and difficult to access. Students, startups, and research laboratories need practical systems that enable experimentation, prototyping, and innovation without excessive cost.

FYBOTICS aims to bridge that gap with engineering-driven products and services designed for real-world use.
